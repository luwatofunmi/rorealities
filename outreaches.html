ReachOut Campaigns

ReachOut Campaigns is a movement which started in 2007 with the aim to reach every man, in his world and in his language, with the good news of hope and change — through the free distribution of Rhapsody of Realities.

Every year, during national holidays and celebrations in nations around the world, ReachOut Campaigns partners organize the free distribution of Rhapsody of Realities to targeted communities. Some distributions are heralded by major carnivals and festivities; others, though marked quietly, are equally impactful in changing lives.

Mission Trips

Right now, there are about 65.6 million displaced people in the world — about one in every 170 persons uprooted by war, persecution, or political upheaval. About 80% of the world's refugees are women and children, who are more vulnerable still.

Rhapsody Mission Trips reach the needy and less privileged with the free distribution of Rhapsody of Realities and relief materials — bringing hope, joy, and salvation to souls who may have never heard the Gospel preached.
