About the Author

Pastor Chris Oyakhilome

D.Sc., D.D. — President of LoveWorld Inc. and of Christ Embassy, and author of the world's #1 daily devotional.

Rev. Chris Oyakhilome D.Sc., D.Sc., D.D., the President of LoveWorld Inc., a dynamic, multifaceted, global ministry, is the author of Rhapsody of Realities, the world's #1 daily devotional, and more than 30 other books. He's a dedicated minister of God's Word whose message has brought the reality of the divine life to the hearts of many.

Atmosphere For Miracles

Billions have been affected by his television broadcast, Atmosphere For Miracles, which brings God's divine presence right into people's homes. The scope of his television ministry extends throughout the world, with LoveWorld satellite television networks delivering qualitative Christian programming to a global audience.

Healing School

At the world-renowned Healing School, he manifests the healing works of Jesus Christ and has helped many receive healing through the operation of the gifts of the Spirit.

A divine commission he's fulfilled for over 40 years — reaching the people of the world with God's presence through outreaches, crusades, and platforms that have helped billions experience a victorious and purposeful life in God's Word.
