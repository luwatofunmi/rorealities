Three ways to put your partnership to work.

Every sponsorship below funds a different part of how Rhapsody of Realities reaches the world — choose the one closest to your heart.

Sponsor a Language

With the bestselling daily devotional currently available in over 8,123 languages and over 4,000 dialects, you can sponsor a language of your choice — and make God's Word available to people in the language they understand best.

Sponsor a Language

Sponsor Mission Trips

Our Messenger Angel, "Rhapsody of Realities," reaches out to the needy and less privileged through Mission Trips, with free distribution of the devotional and relief materials to displaced and underserved communities.

Sponsor Mission Trips

Sponsor ReachOut Campaigns

Rhapsody of Realities brings a fresh perspective from God's Word every day, featuring the day's topic, a theme scripture, the message, and the daily confession — and ReachOut Campaigns is how it reaches entire communities at once.

Sponsor ReachOut Campaigns
Not sure which to pick?

Partner with Rhapsody, generally.

If you'd rather give without earmarking it to one specific program, a general partnership lets the team direct your support to where it's needed most that month.
